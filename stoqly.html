Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't

Stoqly keeps inventory simple. No bloated menus, no confusing setups — just the tools that matter for day-to-day stock management.

🏪

Storage Areas

Organise your inventory into named storage areas — shelves, fridges, storerooms, floor sections. Each area is independent and fully searchable.

  • Up to 5 areas on the free plan
  • Unlimited areas on Pro
  • Custom names, icons, and colours
  • Instant search across all areas
📋

Inventory Items

Add items with photos, barcodes, units of measure, categories, cost price, and reorder levels. Everything in one place, no spreadsheets needed.

  • Unlimited items on all plans
  • Custom units of measure (kg, litres, pcs, boxes…)
  • Cost and value tracking
  • Low-stock threshold per item
  • Batch and expiry date tracking
📷

Barcode Scanner

Scan any product barcode with your iPhone camera. Supported on all common symbologies — EAN, UPC, QR, Code128, ITF14, and more.

  • No external hardware needed
  • Manual entry fallback
  • Instant item lookup or creation
  • Scan to count, scan to find
📊

Dashboard & Analytics

See the health of your entire inventory at a glance — total value, low-stock items, recent activity, and trend insights.

  • Total inventory value
  • Low-stock and out-of-stock counts
  • Category breakdown
  • Activity feed with timestamps
  • Advanced charts on Pro
🔔

Low-Stock Alerts

Set a minimum quantity for any item and Stoqly will send a push notification the moment stock falls below it — so you never run out unexpectedly.

  • Per-item threshold settings
  • Push notifications via system
  • Alert summary on dashboard
  • Works in the background

  • Real-time sync across devices

Invite staff members to your workspace. Control who can view, edit, or manage inventory without sharing your account credentials.

  • CSV export (free)

Run stock counts directly in the app. Scan or tap items to record actual quantities, and let Stoqly flag any discrepancies instantly.

Your data belongs to you. Stoqly does not share inventory data with third parties, and all cloud data is secured under your account credentials.

FAQ

Common questions

Can't find your answer? Contact support →

Is Stoqly really free?
Yes — the free plan is genuinely free, with no time limit and no credit card required. You can manage up to 5 storage areas and unlimited items entirely for free. Ads are shown to keep the free tier sustainable. Upgrade to Pro to remove ads and unlock cloud sync, team access, and advanced reports.
What happens to my data if I cancel Pro?
Your data stays intact. You'll lose access to Pro-only features (cloud sync, team access, PDF export) but all your items and storages remain accessible on the free plan. If you have more than 5 storage areas, you can still view them — you just won't be able to add new ones until you're back below the limit.
Does it work offline?
Yes. All your inventory data is stored locally on your device, so Stoqly works fully without a network connection. On Pro, any changes you make offline are queued and automatically synced to the cloud the next time your device is online.
How do I invite team members?
On the Pro plan, go to the Storages tab → tap the team icon in the top right → Invite Member. Enter their email address, choose their role (Member or Owner), and they'll receive an invite. Once accepted, they'll see and update the same live inventory as you.
Can I import my existing stock list?
Yes. Stoqly supports bulk import from CSV and Excel (.xlsx) files. Go to any storage area → Import → choose your file → map the columns. The importer is flexible — it can handle different column orders and will skip duplicates automatically.
What barcode formats are supported?
Stoqly's scanner supports EAN-8, EAN-13, UPC-E, Code 39, Code 93, Code 128, ITF-14, Data Matrix, Aztec, PDF417, and QR codes. If scanning isn't available on your device, you can always enter barcodes manually.
How is my data secured?
Stoqly uses Firebase Authentication and Firestore with strict per-user security rules — your data is completely isolated from other users. We never share your inventory data with third parties. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings → Profile → Delete Account.
Is there a web or Android version?
Currently Stoqly is iOS only (iPhone). A web dashboard and Android version are on the roadmap. If you'd like to be notified when they launch, drop us a message.
How do subscriptions work?
Pro is a monthly ($4.99/month) or annual ($39.99/year) subscription managed entirely by Apple. You can cancel anytime through iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions — no need to contact us. You keep access until the end of the billing period.
I have a problem or suggestion — how do I reach you?
Use the Support page or email us directly. We're a small indie team and aim to respond within 24–48 hours.
