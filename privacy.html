Your inventory data belongs to you. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

1. About This Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Smart Inventory ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our iOS application, Smart Inventory (the "App"). By using the App you agree to the practices described here.

2. Information We Collect

Account information

  • Email address and password (when you register with email/password)
  • Google account display name and email (when you sign in with Google)
  • A unique user ID generated by Firebase Authentication

Inventory data you create

  • Storage locations, item names, SKUs, barcodes, quantities, unit costs, and notes you enter into the App
  • Inventory count records and adjustment history

Usage and diagnostic data

  • App events and feature usage (via Firebase Analytics) to help us understand how the App is used
  • Crash reports and diagnostic logs (via Firebase Crashlytics) to identify and fix bugs

Advertising identifiers Free tier only

  • Your device's advertising identifier (IDFA / IDFV) may be used by Google AdMob to serve relevant ads, subject to your App Tracking Transparency (ATT) preference
  • Pro subscribers and users who purchase the "Remove Ads" subscription do not see ads and their advertising identifier is not used for ad targeting

Camera

  • Camera access is used solely for scanning barcodes. We do not capture, store, or transmit photos or video

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account and authenticate your identity
  • To sync your inventory data across your devices via Firebase Firestore
  • To restore your data when you reinstall or sign in on a new device
  • To deliver in-app advertisements (free tier) through Google AdMob
  • To monitor App performance and diagnose crashes via Firebase Crashlytics
  • To understand feature usage and improve the App via Firebase Analytics
  • To process subscription payments (handled entirely by Apple's App Store)

5. Data Storage and Security

Your inventory data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework and, when you are signed in, mirrored to Firebase Firestore hosted on Google Cloud servers. Data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections. We apply industry-standard security measures; however, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. App Tracking Transparency (iOS 14+)

On first launch the App requests permission to track your activity across apps and websites using your device's advertising identifier (IDFA), as required by Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework. If you decline, only non-personalised ads will be shown (or no ads if you are a Pro subscriber). You can change this preference at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

Your account and associated inventory data are retained for as long as your account remains active. To delete your account and all associated cloud data:

  • Email us at karthik@vishuddhi.in with the subject line "Delete My Account"
  • We will permanently delete your Firestore data and Firebase Auth account within 30 days

Uninstalling the App removes locally stored data from your device. Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics data is retained per Google's standard retention policies (up to 14 months for Analytics).

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately and we will delete it promptly.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location you may have rights including:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data (see Section 7)
  • Portability: Export your inventory data via the built-in PDF export feature
  • Opt-out of personalised ads: Decline ATT permission or adjust it in iOS Settings

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below.
